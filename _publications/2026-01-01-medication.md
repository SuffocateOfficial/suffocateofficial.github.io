My favourite drug?  

Let's start talking about weed. Without weed, there would be no "Watch Me Suffocate". No "It's Time To Say Goodybe"-Concept. No "Seasons"-EP. And, of course, no "Dragon's Breath" which kinda was the beginning of it all. This song is basically completely based around weed.

Thanks to weed, which over the years helped me stay focused and have a good time. Never forget the good ol' strains.

One "J" during a night out can make the rest of the night way more interesting. But there's a chance of killing the vibe when smoking with beginners. Then you better roll a spliff. Gotta develop the habit of taking hits of the vape more and more regularly over time.

What would I be without Cocaine. It helps me partying, getting my shit done and it's great having during sex. I love doing blow on a girls ass. Thank's "The Wolf of Wall Street".

Mentioning Alcohol, the one drug making me discover those better, other drugs. Fuck you cause of making me stupid and feeling like shit. You're the whore who stayed with me the whole time and made me make bad decisions.

Then there's LSD. Tab's fault for making me go crazy and ready for the psychward.

MDMA? Well, it just makes you gay. Makes relaxing with friends great. But actually the depression afterwards and paranoia is not worth it. Too much of an emotional carousel.

God damn nicotine. Cigarettes steal my money and take over my life. This bitch!

All this shit's gonna rule my whole world.
